Catalog Coverage Checker FAQ



  1. What is the Catalog Coverage Checker?
    2. The Catalog Coverage Report provides an exhaustive list of all racks and the destinations they can go to, offering users a definitive view on what can land where. The Catalog Coverage Checker includes two views:
    1. Detailed View: Exhaustive list of all IPN/Site combinations and whether they are compatible. If they are not compatible, the list of violated constraints is included.
    2. Service Owner View: Exhaustive list of all RackType/AZ combinations and whether there is at least one IPN/Site combination (belonging to the RackType/AZ requested) that is compatible with each other. The Service Owner View is generated using the Detailed View.
